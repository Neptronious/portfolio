A short note

How I show up to work.

I believe that life is not about finding yourself, but creating yourself. Keeping this in mind I try to constantly learn and grow.

When I set out to explore, I like to keep a child-like attitude — one who is not afraid to fail, yet crazy enough to try out what they believe in. Even if I may be wrong, at least I will learn what does not work.

I find it fun to take radical approaches, build them quickly, try them, and pivot based on the feedback. I just keep pushing the boundaries and try to get the best out of myself. Once I set out to do something, I am fully committed — and that's where I find satisfaction and true joy.
